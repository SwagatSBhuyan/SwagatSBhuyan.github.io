Volunteering

Born and raised in a small township in Assam, India, my family was relatively well off because our locality was financially lacking compared to the rest of India. I am currently an Oracle Volunteer working in collaboration with Team Everest NGO in their program, Readiness Skills for Girls. Here, I am assigned a a college student as a mentee, with I have one-on-one sessions with over the phone where I train her to become ready for the job market in her respective field, and also help her find the best career path that would suit her skills and interests. This volunteering event entails a total of 39 sessions with the mentee, where 3 modules are covered - "Know a Career", "Getting Job Ready" and "Critical and Logical Thinking".
